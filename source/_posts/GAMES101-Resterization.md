## 线代知识点回顾

a = (x1, y1)<sup>T</sup>
b = (x2, y2)<sup>T</sup>
- 点乘：dot(a, b) = |a||b|cosθ = x1\*x2+y1\*y2
- 叉乘：cross(a, b) = (x1\*y1, x2\*y2)<sup>T</sup>
